When Services Make Sense

Where Professional Services adds the most value

These services are designed for institutions that need hands-on support building additional agents, integrations, reporting, and workflow customization beyond the ready-to-use admissions and retention agents.

Website support and experience customization

Improve the front-end student experience with landing-page support, messaging alignment, conversational entry points, and related website updates that help students move toward action.

CRM and SIS integration services

Connect EduLab into the systems where student records, lead routing, communication history, and enrollment workflows already live so teams are not working in disconnected tools.

Admissions and marketing support

Shape the website, landing-page, and advertisement experience around your enrollment goals so messaging, creative, offers, and conversion paths support the campaigns your team is trying to run.

Data analytics and reporting

Design the reporting layer around the metrics leadership and frontline teams actually need, from engagement and response patterns to funnel performance, melt indicators, and intervention signals.

End-to-end integration and operational handoff

Build the working model across websites, forms, communications, systems, staff routing, and follow-through so the implementation supports real teams, not just a technical connection.

Agentic AI workflow automation

Design and implement agentic workflows and trained agents for financial aid, registration, bursar billing, hold resolution, surveys, advancement campaigns, and other high-volume campus outreach while keeping human judgment where it matters most.

How It Works

Start with a planning call, then define the right implementation path

  1. 1. Understand the environment Review your current systems, student journey priorities, staffing model, constraints, and timeline.
  2. 2. Define scope and service mix Decide whether the need is website support, integration work, reporting design, automation, or a broader end-to-end project.
  3. 3. Create the plan Map the technical approach, implementation sequence, responsibilities, and the level of partner involvement required.
  4. 4. Launch and refine Move from planning into execution with a working solution that can be adjusted as institutional needs evolve.

Best fit for

  • Institutions with multiple systems that need to work together
  • Teams that need partner-led implementation, not just software access
  • Organizations with reporting, workflow, or customization requirements beyond a standard rollout

What to bring to the call

  • Your current website, CRM, SIS, and reporting stack
  • The workflow or student journey you want to improve first
  • Any timeline, staffing, security, or operational constraints

Typical service motions

  • Implementation sprint with a rollout plan, ownership model, and configuration checklist for a guided admissions or retention launch.
  • Custom workflow build with workflow design, routing logic, content requirements, and launch scope for a specific university use case.
  • Integration + reporting setup with an integration map, reporting design, and operational handoff model across teams.
