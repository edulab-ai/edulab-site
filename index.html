How It Works

A simple engagement loop for higher-ed teams

  1. 1. Engage Start personalized conversations with prospective, admitted, or enrolled students across the channels they already use.
  2. 2. Understand Capture the questions, hesitation, and recurring friction behind drop-off, melt, or early disengagement.
  3. 3. Route Guide the student to the right next step or hand the conversation to staff when human support is needed.
  4. 4. Learn Use the analytics layer to improve messaging, prioritize outreach, and strengthen the student journey over time.
