Built-In Admissions Agent

Turn silent university website visitors into admissions next steps

Prospective students visit university websites every day and leave without meaningful engagement, personalized guidance, or enough confidence to start applying. EduLab helps institutions capture that intent earlier and move more students toward inquiry, application, or counselor follow-up.

This ready-to-use admissions agent fits naturally into website, communications, and staff handoff workflows without requiring a custom admissions build before launch.

What admissions teams gain

A simple flow from interest to action

  1. 1. Welcome the visitor Start with a natural, low-pressure prompt instead of a dead-end landing page experience.
  2. 2. Understand intent Clarify what the student is exploring, what is blocking them, and what information they actually need.
  3. 3. Guide the next step Move them toward inquiry, application, aid support, event signup, or counselor follow-up.
  4. 4. Share usable context Give admissions teams a better read on readiness, hesitation, and the questions that show up most often.
