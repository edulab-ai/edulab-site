Built-In Retention Agent

Understand student melt and retention risk before it is too late

Admitted students who never enroll in classes and students who stop out after a semester or two often do so without universities having clear visibility into why. EduLab helps surface that friction earlier so teams can intervene with more confidence.

This ready-to-use retention agent supports intervention, routing, and reporting workflows without asking your team to design a custom retention workflow before launch.

Summer melt

Admitted students can drift between acceptance and course registration with no clear reason attached.

Unclear barriers

Late outreach

Without conversational insight, support often arrives after the student has already disengaged.

How EduLab helps

Move from missing context to usable intervention signals

Surface friction points

EduLab identifies the concerns that keep admitted or newly enrolled students from moving forward.

Capture common concerns

See repeated questions about cost, paperwork, schedules, support, belonging, or readiness instead of guessing.

Respond earlier

Support persistence

Keep conversations active through high-risk moments where students need clarity, reassurance, or a staff handoff.

Operational framework

A simple detect-understand-respond loop

  1. 1. Detect Track the questions, hesitation, and pattern changes that signal melt or early persistence risk.
  2. 2. Understand Classify concerns so teams know whether the issue is aid, registration, motivation, confusion, or support access.
  3. 3. Respond Use AI nudges, staff handoff, or targeted outreach to resolve the block before the student fully disengages.
  4. 4. Learn Use the analytics layer to identify recurring barriers and strengthen the student journey over time.
